The Band

Parachutes and Promises is a pop band from Seattle, here to lift you up and steal your heart.

Featuring veterans of the Pacific Northwest music scene, their pop-dance-indie blend of music is right at home alongside artists such as Ed Sheeran, Maroon 5, The Weeknd, and Harry Styles.
